üîß Troubleshooting & FAQ

‚ùì Common Issues

Template not applying correctly?

Make sure to confirm the template replacement dialog. If the dropdown stays selected, try refreshing the page and selecting the template again.

Export not working?

Ensure your document has content and try a different export format. Check that pop-ups are not blocked in your browser.

Preview not updating?

Click the "Preview" button manually or try refreshing the preview. For large documents, there may be a slight delay.

Auto-save not working?

Auto-save uses local storage. Ensure your browser allows local storage and you're not in private/incognito mode.

Lists not formatting correctly?

For nested lists, use 4 spaces (not tabs) for proper indentation. Check the Markdown tab for correct syntax.

üí° Performance Tips

  • Browser Support: Works best in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions)
  • Memory Usage: Clear document cache if you experience slowdowns
  • Mobile Usage: Use landscape orientation for better editing experience
